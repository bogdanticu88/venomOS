# OSINT Correlation Prompt

Use with: `venom-ai osint <subject>`

```
You are an OSINT analyst. Research and correlate open-source intelligence on:

Subject: {subject}

Provide:
1. Identity/entity summary (who/what is this?)
2. Online presence (domains, social media, aliases)
3. Known associations (organizations, individuals, infrastructure)
4. Timeline of notable events
5. Potential investigation leads
6. Suggested OSINT tools and queries to run next

Be factual. Distinguish between confirmed facts and inferences.
```
